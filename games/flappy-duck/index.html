About This Game

Guide your duck through an endless gauntlet of green pipes in this addictive arcade game. Tap or click to flap — every touch sends your duck upward, and gravity pulls it back down. Thread the gaps, rack up points, and chase your best score.

Controls

Click or Tap  Flap wings
Space or W or ↑  Flap wings (keyboard)
R  Restart after game over

Each click or tap sends the duck upward. Let gravity do the rest — time your flaps to thread through the gap in each pair of pipes. Hit a pipe or the ground and it's game over. How many pipes can you clear?
