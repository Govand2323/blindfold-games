About This Game

The classic 3-in-a-row strategy game, right in your browser. Two players take turns placing X and O on a 3×3 grid. Get three in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win. If all 9 squares fill up with no winner, it's a draw.

Controls

Left Click  Place your mark on a square

Player 1 is X, Player 2 is O. Take turns clicking an empty square. First to get three in a row wins. Play as many rounds as you like — click New Game to restart.
