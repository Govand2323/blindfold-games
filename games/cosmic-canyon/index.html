About This Game

Cosmic Canyon is a pixel-art top-down space shooter. Control a starfighter and survive relentless waves of enemies across multiple levels. Each level escalates in difficulty — clear all waves to advance. How long can you last?

Controls

W A S D or ↑ ← ↓ →  Move your ship
Mouse  Aim
Left Click  Shoot

Survive all waves in a level to advance to the next. Enemies get faster and more numerous each wave. Avoid getting hit — you have limited health.
