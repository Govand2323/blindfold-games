About This Game

Classic snake with a twist — the edges wrap, so you never hit a wall. Guide your snake to eat red fruit and grow longer. Collect the golden power-up for a big point bonus, but act fast: it only stays for 5 seconds. The only way to die is running into yourself.

Controls

W A S D or ↑ ← ↓ →  Change direction
R  Restart after game over

Eat red fruit to score 10 points and grow. A golden power-up occasionally appears — grab it for 25 points before it vanishes. The snake wraps around all edges. Avoid running into your own body. On mobile, use the on-screen D-pad to steer.
